Product overview

Give customers a clearer delivery scheduling experience.

DeliveryPulse adds delivery date and time-slot selection to the storefront while giving merchants practical controls for managing availability.

✓

Delivery date selection

Let customers choose an available delivery date before completing their order.

✓

Time-slot options

Offer morning, afternoon, and evening delivery preferences.

✓

Preparation days

Set the minimum number of days required before an order can be delivered.

✓

Blocked dates

Prevent customers from selecting holidays, closed dates, or unavailable delivery days.

✓

Sunday availability control

Disable Sunday delivery when it is not available for the store.

✓

Order details saved

Selected delivery information is stored with the order for merchant reference.

Common use cases

Designed for stores that plan delivery ahead.

DeliveryPulse supports merchants that need customers to choose delivery timing before an order is fulfilled.

01

Local delivery

Collect preferred delivery dates for stores managing their own local delivery routes.

02

Bakery and food orders

Give merchants preparation time before scheduled food or custom-order delivery.

03

Scheduled fulfillment

Help organize orders that must be prepared and delivered on specific dates.

04

Holiday availability

Block dates when the business cannot accept or fulfill delivery requests.

Merchant workflow

Configure delivery availability in three steps.

1

Set preparation time

Choose how many days are required before delivery becomes available.

2

Manage availability

Configure blocked dates, Sunday availability, and selectable time slots.

3

Collect customer choices

Customers select their preferred date and time, and the information is saved with the order.

Available

DeliveryPulse FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do customers select a delivery date?

Yes. Customers can select from the delivery dates made available by the merchant.

Can I block specific dates?

Yes. Merchants can add dates that should not be available for customer selection.

Can I require preparation time?

Yes. DeliveryPulse includes a minimum preparation-days setting.

Are delivery time slots supported?

Yes. Customers can choose from morning, afternoon, and evening time-slot options.
