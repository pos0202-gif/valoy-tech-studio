CountdownBoost FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to write code?

No. Banner settings are configured through the app's Shopify admin interface.

Can I customize the banner colors?

Yes. CountdownBoost includes background and text color settings.

Can the banner link to a promotion?

Yes. Merchants can configure custom button text and a destination link.

Can I disable the banner?

Yes. The banner can be enabled or disabled through the app settings.
