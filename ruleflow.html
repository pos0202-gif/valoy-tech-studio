Product overview

Add practical checkout controls to your Shopify store.

RuleFlow helps merchants define checkout requirements and prevent orders that do not meet configured business rules.

✓

Minimum order amount

Require customers to reach a configured minimum cart subtotal before checkout.

✓

Maximum order amount

Prevent orders from exceeding the maximum amount configured by the merchant.

✓

Blocked products

Select individual products that should not be allowed through checkout.

✓

Blocked collections

Apply checkout restrictions to products belonging to selected collections.

✓

Customer tag restrictions

Restrict checkout based on configured customer tags.

✓

Country restrictions

Prevent checkout when the delivery country matches a blocked country rule.

✓

Require customer login

Require customers to sign in before proceeding through checkout.

✓

Custom error messages

Configure customer-facing messages for order, product, customer, and country restrictions.

Common use cases

Built for merchants with specific checkout requirements.

RuleFlow supports stores that need clearer control over which orders can proceed through checkout.

01

Minimum purchase requirements

Require wholesale, delivery, or promotional orders to reach a selected minimum amount.

02

Restricted product combinations

Prevent selected products or collection items from proceeding through checkout.

03

Customer access control

Apply checkout restrictions based on login status or customer tags.

04

Regional order restrictions

Block checkout for countries that the store cannot support or fulfill.

RuleFlow FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to write code?

No. Rules are configured through the RuleFlow settings page in Shopify admin.

Can I block selected products?

Yes. RuleFlow supports restrictions for selected products and collections.

Can I set minimum and maximum order amounts?

Yes. Merchants can configure both minimum and maximum order amount rules.

Can I customize checkout error messages?

Yes. RuleFlow includes configurable messages for supported validation rules.
